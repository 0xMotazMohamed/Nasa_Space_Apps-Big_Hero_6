About AERA

AERA (Air Environmental Real-Time Analytics) is a web-based platform designed to make air quality information accessible for both the public and researchers.

For the public, AERA helps improve daily life by providing clear and reliable air quality data. Users can:

Check real-time levels of key pollutants such as NO₂, HCHO, and O₃.

View the Air Quality Index (AQI) at their location using GPS, by searching for a city, or by selecting a point or custom area on an interactive map.

Receive forecasts powered by a ConvLSTM deep learning model, with alerts when air quality reaches unsafe levels.

For scientists and researchers, AERA offers a research-grade dashboard that combines satellite data from TEMPO with ground validation from Pandora and OpenAQ. Features include:

Time series analysis and pollutant trends.

Nearest ground station comparisons.

Multi-visual charts and climate impact insights.
